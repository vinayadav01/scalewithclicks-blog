## Step 1: Choose the Right Keywords

One of the biggest SEO mistakes beginners make is targeting keywords that are too competitive.

Instead of trying to rank for broad terms like:

- SEO
- marketing
- Google ranking

Focus on **long-tail keywords**.

Examples:

- how to rank on Google fast
- beginner SEO guide
- how to improve Google rankings
- SEO tips for small businesses

Long-tail keywords are easier to rank for and usually convert better.

---

## How to Find Easy Keywords

You don’t need expensive tools to start.

You can use:

- Google autocomplete
- People Also Ask
- Related searches
- Free keyword tools

Look for keywords with:

- Medium or low competition
- Clear search intent
- Real business relevance

---

## Step 2: Create Helpful Content

Google’s goal is simple:

Show the best answer for a search query.

That means your content must genuinely help users.

Instead of writing shallow 500-word articles, create detailed guides that solve problems.

Your content should include:

- Clear headings
- Examples
- Actionable tips
- Internal links
- FAQs
