## 🚀 Step 1: Define Your Goal

Before creating campaigns, you must decide:

- Do you want leads?  
- Sales?  
- Website traffic?  

👉 Your goal determines your entire strategy.

For most businesses, the best goal is **Lead Generation or Sales**.

<br />

## 🎯 Step 2: Keyword Research

![Keyword Research](/images/google-ads-keywords.jpg)

Keywords decide who sees your ads.

### Focus on high-intent keywords:

- "hire google ads expert"  
- "google ads agency near me"  
- "buy product online"  

### Avoid:

- Broad terms like “marketing”  
- Informational queries  

👉 If your keywords are wrong, your ads will fail.  
👉 Read this: [Why Google Ads Don’t Convert](/blog/google-ads-not-converting)

<br />

## 🧱 Step 3: Campaign Structure

A clean structure improves performance.

### Recommended setup:

- 1 Campaign = 1 Goal  
- 3–5 Ad Groups per campaign  
- Closely related keywords per group  

### Example:

Campaign: Lead Generation  
→ Ad Group 1: Google Ads Services  
→ Ad Group 2: PPC Agency  
→ Ad Group 3: Paid Ads Expert  

<br />

## ✍️ Step 4: Write High-Converting Ads

![Ad Copy](/images/google-ads-copy.jpg)

Your ad copy must match user intent.

### Include:

- Clear headline  
- Benefits (not features)  
- CTA (Call Now, Book Free Call)  

### Example:

❌ “We offer marketing services”  
✅ “Get More Leads with Google Ads – Book Free Strategy Call”

<br />

## 🧲 Step 5: Create a High-Converting Landing Page

![Landing Page](/images/landing-page-example.jpg)

Sending traffic to your homepage is a mistake.

### Your landing page should have:

- Clear headline  
- Strong offer  
- Trust proof (testimonials, results)  
- Simple form or CTA  

👉 A bad landing page = wasted ad spend

<br />

![Conversion Tracking](/images/conversion-tracking.jpg)

If you don’t track conversions:

- You don’t know what’s working  
- You can’t optimize campaigns  

### Track:

- Form submissions  
- Calls  
- Purchases

## 💰 Step 7: Budget & Bidding Strategy

Start simple.

### Recommended:

- Start with ₹1000–₹3000/day (or equivalent)  
- Use “Maximize Conversions”  

### Avoid:

- Overcomplicating bidding early  
- Scaling too fast  

<br />

## 🔄 Step 8: Optimize Regularly

Google Ads is NOT set-and-forget.

### Weekly tasks:

- Pause bad keywords  
- Improve ad copy  
- Adjust bids  
- Analyze conversion data
