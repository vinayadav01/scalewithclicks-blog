## 1 Targeting the Wrong Keywords

![Keyword Mistake](/images/keyword-mistake.jpg)

### The Problem

Choosing broad or irrelevant keywords like:
- "marketing"
- "business growth"
- "ads"

These keywords attract **unqualified traffic**.

### The Result

- High clicks ❌  
- Low conversions ❌  
- Wasted budget ❌  

### The Fix

Focus on **high-intent keywords** like:

- google ads agency for small business  
- lead generation services google ads  
- ppc management services  

👉 These users are ready to take action.

---

## 2 Ignoring Search Intent

![Search Intent](/images/search-intent.jpg)

### The Problem

Not all keywords have the same intent.

Example:
- "what is google ads" → informational  
- "hire google ads expert" → transactional  

### The Result

You're paying for clicks from people who are **not ready to buy**.

### The Fix

Match your ads with **user intent**:

| Intent Type | Strategy |
|------------|---------|
| Informational | Blog content |
| Commercial | Comparison pages |
| Transactional | Service pages |

## 3 No Negative Keywords

![Negative Keywords](/images/negative-keywords.jpg)

### The Problem

Without negative keywords, your ads show for irrelevant searches like:
- free
- jobs
- course

### The Result

You pay for **completely useless clicks**.

### The Fix

Add negative keywords such as:

- free  
- tutorial  
- jobs  
- internship  

This alone can reduce wasted spend by **30–50%**.

---

## 4 Poor Landing Page Experience

![Landing Page Issue](/images/landing-page-issue.jpg)

### The Problem

Even if your ads are good, a bad landing page kills conversions.

Common issues:
- Slow loading speed  
- No clear CTA  
- Too much text  
- Not mobile optimized  

### The Result

- High bounce rate  
- Low conversion rate  

### The Fix

Your landing page must:

- Load in under 3 seconds  
- Have a strong headline  
- Include clear CTA (Book Call / Get Quote)  
- Be mobile-friendly

## 5 Not Tracking Conversions

![Tracking Issue](/images/conversion-tracking.jpg)

### The Problem

If you’re not tracking conversions, you're flying blind.

### The Result

You don’t know:
- Which keyword works  
- Which ad converts  
- Where money is wasted  

### The Fix

Set up:

- Google Ads Conversion Tracking  
- Google Analytics 4  
- Call tracking (if applicable)  

👉 Without data, optimization is impossible.

---

## 6 Weak Ad Copy

![Ad Copy Mistake](/images/ad-copy.jpg)

### The Problem

Generic ads like:
> "We provide the best services"

These don’t stand out.

### The Result

- Low CTR  
- Low Quality Score  
- Higher CPC  

### The Fix

Use **benefit-driven copy**:

- Get 3X More Leads in 30 Days  
- Certified Google Ads Experts  
- Free Strategy Call  

👉 Focus on results, not features.

---

## 7 No Optimization Strategy

![Optimization Issue](/images/optimization.jpg)

### The Problem

Most people set campaigns and forget them.

### The Result

Performance declines over time.

### The Fix

Optimize weekly:

- Pause low-performing keywords  
- Scale winning ads  
- Test new headlines  
- Adjust bids  

👉 Google Ads is not “set and forget” — it’s **test and scale**.
