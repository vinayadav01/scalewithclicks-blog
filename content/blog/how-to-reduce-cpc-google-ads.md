## 🎯 Why CPC Matters

Cost Per Click (CPC) directly impacts:

- Your ad budget  
- Number of clicks you can afford  
- Overall ROI  

Lower CPC = More traffic + More leads for the same budget

---

## 🔍 1. Improve Your Quality Score

Google rewards relevant ads with lower CPC.

### Focus on:
- High CTR (Click Through Rate)  
- Relevant keywords  
- Strong landing page  

👉 If your ads are not converting, read:  
[Why Google Ads Are Not Converting](/blog/google-ads-not-converting)

---

## 🎯 2. Use High-Intent Keywords

Avoid broad keywords like:
- "marketing"  
- "ads"  

Instead, target:
- "hire google ads expert"  
- "google ads agency near me"  

High-intent keywords:
- Convert better  
- Reduce wasted clicks  
- Lower CPC over time  

---

## 🚫 3. Add Negative Keywords

This is one of the most underrated strategies.

### Example:
If you sell services, block:
- free  
- jobs  
- course  

This prevents irrelevant clicks and saves money.

---

## ✍️ 4. Improve Ad Copy (Increase CTR)

Better ads = higher CTR = lower CPC

### Tips:
- Use numbers & benefits  
- Add strong CTA  
- Match user intent  

Example:  
"Get More Leads with Google Ads – Free Strategy Call"

---

## 🧲 5. Optimize Landing Page

![Landing Page Optimization](/images/landing-page-example.jpg)

Google checks your landing page quality.

### Improve:
- Page speed  
- Mobile responsiveness  
- Clear headline  
- Strong CTA  

👉 A poor landing page increases CPC and kills conversions

## 📍 8. Target the Right Location & Device

Don’t waste money everywhere.

### Optimize:
- High-performing locations  
- Mobile vs desktop  

Pause low-performing segments.
